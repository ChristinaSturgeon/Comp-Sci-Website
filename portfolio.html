This is my portfolio!

This is a maze game I made in Scratch with my partner Erin! You use the arrow keys to navigate Harry the Hedgehog through 3 maze levels. If you touch the border of the maze you are sent back to the start of the level. Collect grasshoppers to earn points and try to complete it as fast as you can because you are timed!

This is a dice game I made in MIT App Inventor with my partner Owen! You click the dice to roll it and try to get to a total of 21. The number of rolls you take are recorded and you win when you reach 21 exactly!

This is an interactive fiction game I made using Python in Repl.it with my partner Andrija! You make choices to help a North Korean man named Mal-Chin achieve his quest of getting a cheeseburger from In-n-Out in the United States. He must escape the North Korean government to travel to California. Your choices affect his fate. Please help him in his quest!
